About Villa Martina

This beautiful fully equipped villa with a private pool is located near beaches and golf courses. It has automatic shutters that ensure a restful sleep. The villa is modern and air-conditioned, with three bedrooms, three bathrooms, and a private pool. The open plan living and dining area includes a fully equipped kitchen and is filled with lovely midday and evening sun. There are three bedrooms in total, with two having en-suite bathrooms and access to the balcony. The third bedroom is on the ground floor and has a separate bathroom.

The roof has a large sun terrace with sun loungers and a shaded sitting area, which offers an unobstructed panoramic view. The sun terrace includes an outdoor kitchen with a refrigerator where you can enjoy chilled drinks at sunset. The terrace is equipped with a table, barbecue, deck chairs, and an umbrella. The villa cannot be seen from outside and has a private heated pool. There is also free Wi-Fi and international channels (English, French, German, Spanish, etc.).

The villa is conveniently located near several beaches (Cabo Roig, Campoamor, Punta Prima, La Zenia, Playa Flamenca) and golf courses (Las Ramblas, Campoamor, Villamartin, and Las Colinas), as well as all amenities. Villamartin Plaza, which is 2 km away, has a pharmacy, restaurants, pubs, bank, hairdresser, and a supermarket. The Plaza in Villamartin also offers free live music, ambiance, and performances from one of the many bars and restaurants. Aldi is located at 900 meters.

In just 10 minutes, you can reach the shopping center Zénia Boulevard, which has more than 150 stores and restaurants. It is open daily from 10:00 to 22:00. A few kilometers away is the city of Torrevieja, which has a lively atmosphere. Other nearby cities include Murcia, Elche, Alicante, Orihuela, Mar Menor, and Cartagena. The region has an exceptionally pleasant climate throughout the year.
